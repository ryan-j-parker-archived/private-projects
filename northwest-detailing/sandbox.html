Ceramic coatings

We are pleased to offer ceramic coatings! This treatment is highly effective at blocking UV rays and keeping salt and acid rain from penetrating the pores of your boat, offering your boat great protection from the harsh Northwest elements. After treatment with ceramic coating, a boat's surface becomes very hydrophobic, leading to better performance and less drag in the water, and easier cleaning! You can expect our high-quality ceramic coatings to have a life span of up to three years.
